About Varveris & Associates

Varveris & Associates was founded by Nicholas G. Varveris in August of 1988. Nick is a certified public accountant with over 30 years of experience. His diverse experience includes working with public and private companies in a variety of industries. Varveris & Associates has worked with hardware and software development companies, manufacturers and wholesale distribution companies as well as retail business and service companies.
